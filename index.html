About Me

I am a creative spirit at heart and longtime gaming enthusiast. In 2021 I became a Content Creator in the gaming space, and through this experience I have cultivated skills in content marketing, video editing, live broadcasting, and social media marketing. My desire for tech and creativity is only matched by my love of gaming... I also LOVE foxes.

Why Software Engineering?

After years working in marketing I wanted to turn my passion for tech and gaming industries into a career. This led me to General Assembly's Software Engineering course. While at General Assembly I learned both front-end and back-end development using languages and libraries such as: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap, React, Express, MongoDB, Python and SQL. Using these tools I have created several school projects as well as some of my own personal projects.
